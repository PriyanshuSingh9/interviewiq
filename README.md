# Agentic AI-Powered Mock Interview Platform

## Hackathon Problem Statement

### Background
Job seekers — especially fresh graduates and career switchers — lack access to realistic, personalized interview practice. Existing solutions are either static (pre-recorded questions) or expensive (human coaches), leaving candidates underprepared for the dynamic, unpredictable nature of real interviews.

### The Challenge
Build an agentic AI mock interview platform that autonomously conducts end-to-end mock interviews tailored to a candidate's target role, experience level, and resume. The AI agent should go beyond scripted Q&A — it must think, adapt, and respond like a real interviewer by dynamically generating follow-up questions, detecting weak answers, probing deeper, and providing structured post-interview feedback.

### Core Requirements
The solution should demonstrate agentic behavior across the interview lifecycle:
- **Pre-Interview** — Parse the candidate's resume and job description to generate a personalized interview plan.
- **During Interview** — Adapt questions in real time based on candidate responses, simulate behavioral, technical, and situational rounds.
- **Post-Interview** — Deliver a detailed performance report covering communication, depth of knowledge, confidence signals, and areas of improvement.
